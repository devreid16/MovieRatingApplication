Statement of Faith

We Believe:

  • In the inerrancy of Scripture
  • In one God eternally existing in the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
  • In the Virgin Birth of Jesus
  • In the Deity of Jesus
  • In the death, burial, and resurrection of Jesus for the forgiveness of sin for all who believe
  • That salvation (eternal life with God) is by faith alone in Jesus Christ
  • In the personal return of our Lord Jesus Christ

Effective Discipleship Must Be:

Biblical discipleship is helping your disciple to walk by faith, communicate their faith and multiply their faith.

Relational - 2 Tim 2:2
Inspirational - 2 Tim 4:7
Doctrinal - 2 Tim 1:13
Experiential - Mat 28:18-20


Effective Disciples Should Be:

A disciple is a person who is a pupil or an adherent of the doctrines of another; a follower. The best disciple makers are disciples themselves

Faithful - Ps 37:28
Available - Heb 13:16
Teachable - Heb 5:12
